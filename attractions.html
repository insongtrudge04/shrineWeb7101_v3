Historical Treasures

Casa Cuadrada

Rizal's main residence where his family stayed. It also included a workshop and sleeping quarters for his pupils.

Casa Redonda

An octagonal clinic and dormitory for students, where Rizal treated his many international patients.

Rizaliana Museum

A repository of original relics, writings, and photographs from Rizal's productive four-year stay.

Talisay Shoreline

The tranquil beach where Rizal wrote, thought, and established a community water system.
